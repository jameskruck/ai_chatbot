Your 90-Day Implementation Roadmap

Here's your personalized plan to move from strategy to action.

Phase 1: Research & Planning (Days 1-30)

Week 1: Foundation

Essential Resources:
  • Chatbase documentation
  • Create free trial accounts on 2-3 platforms
  • List your 10 most common customer questions

Weeks 2-4: Deep Dive

Key Activities:
  • Categorize 50+ customer emails by question type
  • Calculate time savings potential
  • Build initial business case with ROI numbers

Phase 2: Pilot Implementation (Days 31-60)

Implementation Checklist:
  • Test 2-3 chatbot platforms with your actual FAQ data
  • Set up basic FAQ responses and escalation paths
  • Run limited pilot with internal team first
  • Gather feedback and iterate

Phase 3: Scale & Optimize (Days 61-90)

Scaling Activities:
  • Launch to broader customer base
  • Monitor key metrics daily
  • Optimize responses based on real usage
  • Plan next phase expansion
Advanced Learning:
  • Intercom's AI insights
  • Zendesk best practices
  • Join chatbot communities and forums
