🤔 Critical Thinking: The Hard Questions

These questions don't have "right" answers. They test whether you can think strategically about complex trade-offs.

Question 1: The Failure Scenario

Jessica implemented the chatbot and 6 months later, customer complaints have increased 30%. Customers say the bot is "frustrating" and "doesn't understand." What went wrong and how would you fix it?

Question 2: The Competitor Response

FashionForward's main competitor just launched an AI chatbot that can handle complex styling advice and personal shopping. Customers are switching brands. How should Jessica respond?

Question 3: The Ethical Dilemma

Your chatbot is so good that customers don't realize they're talking to AI. Some customers share personal financial problems, thinking they're talking to a human. What's your policy?

Question 4: The Scale Challenge

Your successful chatbot now handles 80% of customer inquiries. But the remaining 20% are so complex that human agents feel overwhelmed and undertrained. How do you solve this?
