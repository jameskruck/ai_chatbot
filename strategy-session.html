Part B: Hands-On Implementation

🛠️ Building Your FashionForward Chatbot

Based on your study group's collaborative analysis, you're now going to build a functional chatbot prototype using Chatbase. This will handle the specific FAQ types your group identified as highest priority.

Step 1: Set Up Your Chatbot Platform

We'll use Chatbase - a user-friendly AI chatbot platform perfect for retail FAQ bots.

  1. Go to chatbase.co
  2. Sign up for a free account
  3. Click "Create Chatbot"
  4. Choose "Website" as your source type
💡 Study Group Tip

From Priya: "I've used Chatbase before - it's much easier than building from scratch. The key is getting your training data really clean before you upload it."

Step 2: Train Your Bot with FashionForward FAQs

Upload the FashionForward FAQ knowledge base to train your chatbot:

📋 FashionForward FAQ Dataset

From Marcus: "These are based on real customer service patterns I see in retail - focus on the top 3 categories first."

Sizing Questions (34% of volume)
  • Q: What sizes do you carry?
    A: We offer sizes XS, S, M, L, XL, and XXL. Our shirts have a modern fit - if you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.
  • Q: Do your shirts run large or small?
    A: Our shirts fit true to size with a modern cut. Check our size guide for exact measurements. If you prefer a looser fit, consider going up one size.
  • Q: What's your return policy for wrong sizes?
    A: We offer free exchanges within 30 days! Just keep the tags on and we'll send you a different size at no extra cost.
Order Status (26% of volume)
  • Q: When will my order ship?
    A: Orders typically ship within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a tracking email as soon as your package is on its way!
  • Q: How can I track my order?
    A: Check your email for a tracking link, or use your order number on our order status page. Most orders arrive within 5-7 business days.
  • Q: My tracking hasn't updated in days. What's wrong?
    A: Sometimes tracking can be delayed during updates. If it's been more than 7 days since shipping, please contact our support team and we'll investigate immediately.
Returns & Exchanges (18% of volume)
  • Q: How do I return or exchange an item?
    A: Easy! Visit our returns page, enter your order number, and we'll email you a prepaid return label. Returns are free within 30 days.
  • Q: Can I exchange for a different design?
    A: Absolutely! As long as it's within 30 days and the item has tags attached, you can exchange for any item of equal or lesser value.

To add this data to Chatbase:

  1. Copy the FAQ content above
  2. In Chatbase, go to "Data Sources"
  3. Choose "Text" and paste the FAQ content
  4. Click "Train" to teach your bot these responses

Step 3: Set FashionForward's Brand Voice

Configure your chatbot to match FashionForward's friendly, casual brand personality:

🎭 Brand Voice Settings

From Sarah: "This is critical for ROI - brand-aligned bots see 25% higher customer satisfaction in my consulting experience."

Personality Instructions (copy this into Chatbase):

You are FashionForward's helpful customer service assistant. You should be:

  • Friendly and conversational (never formal or corporate)
  • Enthusiastic about sustainable fashion
  • Quick to offer solutions and alternatives
  • Supportive and understanding of customer concerns

Always end responses with an offer to help further. Use emojis sparingly but appropriately. If you can't answer something, warmly direct them to human support.

Step 4: Test Your Chatbot

Test your chatbot with these sample customer questions to ensure it's working properly:

🧪 Test Scenarios

From Marcus: "Always test the emotional scenarios - angry customers are where most bots fail."

Test 1: "What size should I order if I usually wear a medium?"

Expected: Sizing guidance with recommendation to check size guide

Test 2: "When will my order arrive?"

Expected: Shipping timeline and tracking information

Test 3: "I need to return this shirt"

Expected: Return process with 30-day policy information

Test 4: "I'm really frustrated - my package is lost!"

Expected: Empathetic response and escalation to human support
