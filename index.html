Course Overview

Welcome! This course takes a unique approach to learning AI chatbot strategy. Instead of traditional lectures, you'll work through a real business scenario using Ivey's case method.

Your Virtual Study Group: You'll collaborate with three virtual MBA peers, each bringing different expertise:

  • Sarah Chen - Finance MBA from McKinsey
  • Marcus Rodriguez - Retail operations veteran

Why This Works: Collaboration across diverse expertise develops strategic thinking and real-world implementation skills.
