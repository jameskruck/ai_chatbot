Welcome & Introduction

Let’s start by exploring why chatbots matter in retail and what you’ll learn today.

Learning Outcome

  • Create a basic AI chatbot prototype using Chatbase.

Why Can’t They Do This Already?

Many retail leaders feel overwhelmed by chatbot technology. Let’s clear up some common misconceptions:

  • ❌ “I don’t have technical skills.” Chatbase makes it simple—no coding required!
  • ❌ “My store doesn’t have enough online traffic.” Chatbots save staff time by handling repetitive inquiries, no matter your traffic.
  • ❌ “It’s too expensive or complicated.” Many chatbot tools are free to start.

Pro Tips for Success:

  • ✅ Start with 3–5 of your most common FAQs.
  • ✅ Test your chatbot yourself to ensure clarity and accuracy.
  • ✅ Keep answers short, friendly, and direct—like talking to a real customer.

Why Chatbots?

Chatbots can reduce repetitive questions, improve response times, and boost customer satisfaction.
