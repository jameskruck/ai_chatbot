Executive Case Study: FashionForward Strategic Decision

📋 The Decision

April 8, 2024, Toronto - Jessica Martinez, CEO of FashionForward T-Shirts, stares at 127 unanswered customer emails. Her board meeting is Thursday (3 days away), and directors expect her recommendation for solving the customer service crisis threatening their growth trajectory.

🚨 The Crisis

4.2 → 3.1 Customer satisfaction (6 months)
8 → 31 Response time (hours)
$8.7M 2023 Revenue at risk

⚖️ Three Strategic Options

🤖 AI Chatbot

Cost: $87K (2 years)

Timeline: 6-8 weeks

Pros: Scalable, 24/7, handles 65% of tickets

Cons: Limited empathy, setup complexity

👥 Team Expansion

Cost: $256K (2 years)

Timeline: 4-6 weeks

Pros: Personal touch, brand alignment

Cons: Higher costs, hiring challenges

🏢 Outsourcing

Cost: $383K (2 years)

Timeline: 3-4 weeks

Pros: Professional, immediate capacity

Cons: Expensive, less brand control

📊 Critical Data Points

1,850 monthly customer tickets
34% are sizing questions
$156K quarterly revenue at risk
28% customer retention (down from 34%)

💼 Board Expectations

"We can't afford to lose customers over service issues when acquisition costs are rising." — Jennifer Walsh, Board Member
"Customer satisfaction is a leading indicator. We need improvement in Q2 or we'll face funding problems." — Robert Kim, Board Chair

🤝 Strategic Consultation Session

You're now Jessica's strategic advisor. Work with an AI business consultant to analyze the three options and develop a compelling board recommendation. Consider financial impact, strategic alignment, implementation risks, and long-term scalability.

🎯 Your Analysis Framework:

  • 📈 Financial Analysis: ROI, cash flow impact, risk assessment
  • ⚖️ Strategic Trade-offs: Cost vs. quality vs. scalability
  • 🕐 Implementation Reality: Timeline, resources, change management
  • 🏆 Competitive Advantage: How customer service can differentiate
  • 🎤 Board Presentation: Clear recommendation with compelling rationale

⏰ The Clock is Ticking: Jessica presents to the board in 3 days. What's your recommendation?
